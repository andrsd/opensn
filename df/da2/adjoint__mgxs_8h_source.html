8/**
9 * A wrapper class for obtaining multi-group cross section data for
10 * adjoint simulations.
11 *
12 * In adjoint simulations, the transfer and production matrices are transposed.
13 * While the respective matrices could be queried by simply flipping the
14 * indices, access attempts in this fashion are quite costly. Rather, this
15 * class precomputes and stores these transpose operators. Along with this, a
16 * reference to an instance of MultiGroupXS is stored. In this class, accessors
17 * for vector data call the respective accessor from MultiGroupXS and accessors
18 * for transfer and production matrices access the respective transposed data
19 * stored in this class.
